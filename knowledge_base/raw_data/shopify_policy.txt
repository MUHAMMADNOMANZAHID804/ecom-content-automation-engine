SHOPIFY LISTING POLICY & MARKETPLACE CONNECT GUIDE — Knowledge Base v4.0
Source: Official Shopify Marketplace Connect & AUP Documentation

=== SHOPIFY PRODUCT LISTING LIMITS ===
Shopify does NOT impose strict numerical limits on product listings for most plans.
Compliance depends on following CONTENT and CATEGORY policies — not quantity.
Especially sensitive: health products, supplements, adult content require extra care.

=== KEY POLICY RESOURCES ===
1. Shopify Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) — what you can and cannot sell
2. Prohibited Products documentation — specific banned categories
3. Account protection guidelines — what causes account suspension

=== EXTERNAL CHANNEL POLICIES (STRICTER THAN SHOPIFY ITSELF) ===
Google Shopping: strict prohibited items list, accurate GTIN required
Facebook/Instagram: additional ad content restrictions apply
TikTok Shop: creator compliance requirements
ALWAYS check channel-specific policies when connecting external channels.

=== MARKETPLACE CONNECT — CUSTOMIZE PRODUCT LISTINGS ===
Three customization options:
1. METAFIELDS: Add custom fields not captured in Shopify admin
2. ATTRIBUTE MAPPING: Customize what product info is published to marketplace listings
3. TITLE VARIABLES: Use formula values to customize listing titles

=== SUPPORTED METAFIELD TYPES (sync with Marketplace Connect) ===
SUPPORTED:
- Date and Time, Date, Decimal, Dimension, Integer
- Multi Line text, Single Line text, Collection
- URL, True or False, Color

NOT SUPPORTED (will not sync):
- JSON, Rich Text, Weight, Volume, Mixed Reference
- Money, Rating, Page, Metaobject, Files, Product Variant

If you have duplicate metafield names, only the FIRST one created will sync.

=== ATTRIBUTE MAPPING (Marketplace Connect) ===
Lets you customize what Shopify catalog information is published to marketplace listings.
All CORE Shopify product metafields are mapped automatically after setup.
Some marketplaces require info NOT in default Shopify admin (e.g., bullet points, specific dimensions).
Steps: Shopify Admin → Apps → Marketplace Connect → Mapping → Select marketplace → Save.
After mapping: custom metafields auto-populate when you upload new products — streamlines listing.

=== TITLE CUSTOMIZATION WITH VARIABLES ===
Default: Marketplace Connect uses your Shopify product name as listing title.
Changing product name automatically changes marketplace listing title.
Custom formula examples:
- "NEW {name}" → inserts "NEW" before product name
- "{vendor} {name}" → combines vendor attribute + product name
Enter formula in Title column in single edit mode or bulk edit mode.

=== SEO CHARACTER LIMITS (Best Practice) ===
Product Title: 70 characters max (Google Shopping SEO best practice)
Meta Description: 320 characters max
Tags: 255 characters total (comma-separated)
Description: no hard limit, 300-1000 words recommended for SEO

=== PROHIBITED CONTENT (AUP) ===
Counterfeit or replica products
Items that infringe intellectual property rights
Weapons without proper licensing
Regulated substances (prescription drugs, certain supplements without approval)
Adult content without prior approval
Misleading or deceptive product descriptions
Items that violate any applicable law

=== RESTRICTED CATEGORIES (extra documentation required) ===
Health products and supplements: FDA compliance documentation
Firearms and accessories: strict compliance and licensing
Alcohol: age verification required
CBD/Cannabis: region-specific restrictions apply
Medical devices: FDA compliance required

=== HIGH-PERFORMING SHOPIFY PATTERNS ===
Short keyword-first title + accurate meta description = best Google Shopping performance
Metafield mapping automates multi-marketplace sync — set once, apply to all products
HTML-structured description with headers improves mobile readability
Accurate product attributes (GTIN, brand, condition) reduce disapprovals on Google
Separate metafields for each marketplace's required fields = cleaner listings
