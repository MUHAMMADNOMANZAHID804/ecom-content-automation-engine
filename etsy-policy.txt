ETSY LISTING POLICY & OPTIMIZATION GUIDE — Knowledge Base v4.0
Source: Official Etsy Advertising & Seller Policy Documentation

=== TITLE BEST PRACTICES (CRITICAL FOR ADVERTISING) ===
Keep titles short and sweet — ideally 40 characters or less for advertising compliance.
Brief, clear listing titles are preferable over a long, confusing jumble of keywords.
Lead with your most relevant keywords (front-load).
Example GOOD: "Women's Red Silk Floral Blouse" (30 chars — ad compliant)
Example BAD: "Women's blouse, red blouse, floral blouse, silk blouse, women's clothing, gift"
Avoid ALL UPPERCASE letters in titles.
Title and thumbnail photo must accurately describe the item including all variations.
Shoppers who click an ad and find it doesn't match expectations = lost trust.

=== CHARACTER LIMITS ===
TITLE: 140 characters maximum (ideal: 40 characters for advertising compliance)
DESCRIPTION: 15,000 characters maximum
TAGS: Exactly 13 tags, each maximum 20 characters

=== PHOTO REQUIREMENTS (ADVERTISING COMPLIANCE) ===
Most advertising platforms (Google, Pinterest, Facebook, Instagram) prohibit:
- Watermarks or other promotional text on images
- Cluttered backgrounds or added borders that distract from the item
Product must be the STAR of the image, not your logo.
First image MUST show the actual physical item the buyer will receive — not a rendering or stock photo.
For personalized/custom items (monogrammed, engraved): first image must show a FINISHED customized item, not blank product or placeholder text like "Your Text Here".

=== CREATIVITY STANDARDS — FOUR SELLER CATEGORIES (MUST ACCURATELY REPRESENT) ===
1. MADE BY A SELLER: Physical items crafted, altered, or assembled by a seller, by hand or using tools they own (3D printer, Cricut machine)
2. DESIGNED BY A SELLER: Original designs by a seller (or seller-prompted AI art) offered as digital download or produced/printed by a third-party
3. SOURCED BY A SELLER: Items that enable buyer creativity — craft supplies, party decorations, print-on-demand items
4. HANDPICKED BY A SELLER: Vintage items (20+ years old), items from nature, curated collections

Buyers see how your item is categorized in the listing — accuracy is mandatory.

=== PRODUCTION PARTNER RULES ===
A production partner is a third party who helps turn your original designs into physical products.
Must disclose production partners on any applicable listings.
Qualifying production partners: print-on-demand services, cut-and-sew manufacturers, contract manufacturers, specialized production services.
NOT qualifying as production partners: yourself, shop members, pets, materials suppliers, limited assistance providers (workshop that fired your ceramic mug = not a production partner).
Maintain complete shop About section: list all shop members, outside production partners, recent photos of creative process and workspace.

=== PROHIBITED ITEMS (8 CATEGORIES) ===
1. Alcohol, Tobacco, Drugs, Drug Paraphernalia, Medical Drugs and Devices, Items making Medical Claims
2. Animal Products and Human Body Parts and Fluids
3. Dangerous Items: Hazardous Materials and Devices, Recalled Items, Weapons
4. Hate Items: Items that Promote, Support, or Glorify Hatred
5. Illegal Items, Items Promoting Illegal Activity, and Highly Regulated Items
6. Internationally Regulated Items
7. Nudity and Sexual Content
8. Violent Items: Items that Promote, Support, or Glorify Violence

=== PROHIBITED PRACTICES ===
Listing mass-produced items as handmade
Listing items as "vintage" if less than 20 years old
Using trademark/brand names you don't own
Using digital mock ups instead of actual product photography as first image
Inaccurate categorization of seller role

=== TAG RULES (13 TAGS EXACTLY) ===
Use ALL 13 tags — blank tags waste search visibility.
Multi-word tags preferred (how buyers actually search).
Use buyer language, not seller/industry jargon.
No competitor brand names.
Tag strategy: 4 product type tags + 3 use-case tags + 3 material tags + 2 occasion/audience tags = 13 total.
Tags are NOT visible to buyers — purely for search algorithm.

=== DESCRIPTION STRUCTURE (STORY-DRIVEN) ===
1. Emotional opening hook — connects with buyer identity
2. Accurate product description including all variations offered
3. Materials, dimensions, weight
4. Personalization options (if applicable)
5. Production time + shipping note
6. Care instructions

=== ADVERTISING COMPLIANCE (GOOGLE, PINTEREST, FACEBOOK, INSTAGRAM) ===
Most ad platforms have strict policies about what can be promoted.
Remove any items that violate Etsy's policies before advertising.
Prohibited items cannot be advertised on external platforms.
Intellectual property violations will be flagged by ad platforms.
Accurate item descriptions are required — no misleading titles.

=== HIGH-PERFORMING ETSY PATTERNS ===
Short emotional title (40 chars) + front-loaded keyword = best ad performance
All 13 tags used = maximum Etsy search visibility
Story-first description builds buyer connection and brand trust
Accurate first photo (actual item, not mockup) = higher conversion + fewer returns
Production partner disclosure = builds trust, prevents policy flags
